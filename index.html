🤖 FAQ Chatbot

NLP-based question matching using Cosine Similarity & TF Vectorization

👋 Hi! I'm your FAQ assistant. Ask me anything about orders, returns, payments, or support — or click a topic above!

⚙️ NLP Techniques Used

  • Tokenization — splits and cleans text into individual words
  • Stopword Removal — filters out common words (is, the, a…)
  • TF Vectorization — builds word-frequency vectors from FAQ corpus
  • Cosine Similarity — finds the most semantically similar FAQ to your query
  • Confidence Threshold — rejects low-score matches gracefully
